The Program

8 weeks. Zero to portfolio-ready.

Every week builds on the last — from how to think about design, to a published case study you can show a client or an employer.

01

Design Thinking & The Big Picture

What UX actually means, the design process, and Nigerian app examples — Kuda, PiggyVest, Paystack, Flutterwave.

02

User Research

Interviews, surveys, personas. Designing for real people, not assumptions.

03

Information Architecture & User Flows

Sitemaps and user flows — how to structure an app before touching Figma.

04

Wireframing

Low-fidelity design in Figma. Structure before visual design. Your first real screens.

05

Visual Design Principles

Typography, colour theory, spacing, grids, hierarchy. Where design gets beautiful.

06

Figma Deep Dive

Components, auto layout, design systems. Figma from beginner to confident.

07

Prototyping & Usability Testing

Clickable prototypes, real user testing, and iterating on actual feedback.

08

Portfolio & Case Study

Present your project, publish your case study, and graduate with proof of work.
